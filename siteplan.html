WeldPath — Website Planning Document

W05 Project: Website Planning Document

1. Site Name

WeldPath — a clear, memorable name indicating a guided path into welding fundamentals and careers.

(Optional domain): weldpath.org (availability not required for this class)

2. Site Purpose

The site introduces common welding processes (MIG, TIG, Stick, FCAW), safety and PPE, and career pathways. It offers quick comparisons, beginner guidance, and a simple request-info form.

3. Scenarios

  • “I’m brand-new—what’s the difference between MIG and TIG and which should I learn first?”
  • “What certifications do I need to get hired in structural steel welding?”
